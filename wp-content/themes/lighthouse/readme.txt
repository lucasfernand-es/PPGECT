== Description ==

With search engines in mind such as Google and Bing we created the SEO optimised responsive WordPress theme Lighthouse. It is a perfect responsive and gorgeous SEO friendly and SEO optimized WordPress blog theme. It is mainly created for blogging and writing but it is indeed a multipurpose theme, beside bloggers, writers and authors a lot of people can use it, you can for example use it as a personal blog, fashion blog, travel blog, flat, journal, magazine, news website, affiliate marketing site, conference, newspaper, podcast, developers, adsense, actors, ads, book writers, school and education, biography,  article feed, landing page, journalist blog, business, photography and more. Lighthouse is best described as clean, creative, light, reading friendly, minimal, modern, white, blank, pixel perfect design, awesome, simple, elegant, minimalistic and most of all modern. It works with almost every single plugin so use it to create your event website, one page / single page, coach website or whatever you have in mind! Lighthouse is fully customizable and has a lot of feature, such as: Full width template, right sidebar template, Widget support, Footer widgets, custom colors, custom logo, custom header text and tagline, custom background image, custom header image, favicon and site icon, custom header text color, custom background color, custom theme color, fast loadtime, custom header background colour and of course custom menu. One of the unique things about the theme is it's loadtime, it is insanely fast and quick and takes milliseconds to load. If you are a writer, food blogger, author or even a journalist then Lighthouse is perfect for you - Give your readers experience with beautiful articles featuring clean typography and good readability.

== Installation ==
	
1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.
